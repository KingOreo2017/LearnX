Unit 1 Lesson 1

Once upon a time, there was a girl named Druzella. She was captured by the evil snow king, Frost, to stop her from marrying her prince, Henry. Prince Henry was on a journey to save her but was stopped by many obstacles on his path.

This is normal 1 by 1 addition. Using the given number line, solve these problems and then click submit to check your answer.

1. Prince Henry was heading into the swap when he was stopped by two ogers. The first oger demanded he pay 3 gold to pass. The Second demanded he pay 6 gold. How much gold does Prince Henry have to pay?

2. Prince Henry arrived at the gates of the Ice Palace. He had to climb over the nearby fence to enter. He used a 2 foot stand to be the same height as the fense. to climb over. Prince Henry is 6 feet tall. How tall is the fense?
